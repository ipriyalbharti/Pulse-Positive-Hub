Check Your Stress Level

Answer these 10 questions honestly to know your stress level.

1. How often do you feel anxious?

2. How often do you feel tired without physical reason?

3. Do you experience headaches or muscle tension?

4. Do you have trouble sleeping?

5. Do you get irritated easily?

6. Do you overthink situations often?

7. Do you find it hard to relax?

8. Do you feel pressure at work or studies frequently?

9. Do you experience mood swings?

10. Do you feel overwhelmed with responsibilities?
