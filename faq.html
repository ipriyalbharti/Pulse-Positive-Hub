Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions to help you understand how Positive Pulse Hub works.

What is Positive Pulse Hub?

Positive Pulse Hub is your stress management companion that offers relaxation tools, motivational quotes, and a stress tracker.

Is this platform free?

Yes! Positive Pulse Hub is completely free to use for stress relief and mindfulness exercises.

How do I use the stress tracker?

Click on “Get Started” on the homepage, answer the questions, and receive personalized tips based on your stress level.

Can I access Positive Pulse Hub on mobile?

Yes! The website is mobile-friendly and works smoothly on any device.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required. You can access all features without signing up.
