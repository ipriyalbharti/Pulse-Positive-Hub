All About Stress

Learn what stress is, its impact, and what actions are taken globally and in India.

What is Stress?

Stress is your body's natural response to challenges or pressure. Short-term stress can help you focus, but chronic stress can harm mental and physical health.

Global Impact

1 in 4 people worldwide may experience stress-related issues. Chronic stress contributes to heart disease, diabetes, and sleep disorders globally.

Actions Taken Internationally

WHO provides mental health guidelines. Many countries implement mindfulness programs in schools and workplace wellness programs.

Stress in India

High stress among youth and urban adults. Government initiatives like Manodarpan and NMHP provide counseling and awareness.

NGOs & Organizations

Art of Living, Mind Matters India, LiveLoveLaugh Foundation, and others provide workshops and community support for stress relief.

Tips & Resources

Daily habits: breathing exercises, meditation, short breaks, exercise, sleep hygiene, journaling. Online apps: Headspace, Calm, Insight Timer.
